Anti-barking device (in czech Antištěkač)

I would describe this device as the helper to calm down a noisy dog. If a dog barks loudly the device will register noisy dog and will make a noise.
This project was a part of BI-ARD class (authentication required). I chose this topic to implement because it required quite a few external devices to use (aka shields)

Shields I used

  • breadboard (for easy development)
  • buzzer
  • microphone
  • LED light

How it actually works

Well as already mentioned this device will make uncomfortable noise for the loudly barking dog and will continue doing it until it's quite. However if a dog i barking for too long (configurable constant) it will stop making noise (most likely the dog is barking for good reason).

More detailed look at shields

Well there a lot of external shields you can use in your next arduino project. But I will have look only on 3 of them (which I used here).

Buzzer
This shield is responsible for making a noise. You can configure the frequency on which you want to launch the sound. You can have a look at buzzer shield on the shop.
Breadboard
This is the helper tool to connect your wires during the development with ease. You can find a breadboard on the shop.
Microphone
Mic will help you to register the sound and it's loudness. You can have a look at Mic shield on the shop.
LED light
It's simply a LED light which you can use for various sates of your device. For example in this case the red light will be activated when the dog is barking. You can buy the LED light on the shop.
